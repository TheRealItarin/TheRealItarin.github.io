The 5 level of infrastructure as code

I was looking forward to attending this conference, because I just had hand on experience with this topic on my internship place. While it was an interesting experience, sadly they didn't talk about the generic concept too much. It was more like "This is how we implemented infrastructure as code in our company" rather than "This is what infrastructure as code is". They provided only examples for their company's ecosystem, not even a glance to other platforms. While Azure and Microsoft infrastructure is very extensive, I felt that the conference could have provided more insight into the broader applications and benefits of infrastructure as code. To summurize this tech and meet, it was a nice experience but the whole prezentation felt like they are not talking about the topic they stated, and they were here only to headhunt future employees for their company.
