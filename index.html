Attended events and conferences

The 5 level of infrastructure as code

I was looking forward to attending this conference, because I just had hand on experience with this topic on my internship place. While it was an interesting experience, sadly they didn't talk about the generic concept too much. It was more like "This is how we implemented infrastructure as code in our company" rather than "This is what infrastructure as code is". They provided only examples for their company's ecosystem, not even a glance to other platforms. While Azure and Microsoft infrastructure is very extensive, I felt that the conference could have provided more insight into the broader applications and benefits of infrastructure as code. To summurize this tech and meet, it was a nice experience but the whole presentation felt like they are not talking about the topic they stated, and they were here only to headhunt future employees for their company.

Job market fare at Fontys

During my exchange study program in the Netherlands, I had the opportunity to explore the job market and gain insights into the local industry. However it was quite the sad realization that most job expect me to speak Dutch even though in IT everyone speaks English. Still the experience was invaluable in understanding the demands and expectations of the professional world.

Project fare at Fontys

At the end of the exchange program, we presented our group project, and than later showcased it in a project fare, where multiple companies and teachers were present. They asked about the implementation details and challenges faced during the project. It was a great experience and provided valuable networking opportunities.

Inauguration Cyber Defense Factory

This conference was a great opportunity to learn about the latest developments in cyber defense and network security. Also was a great opportunity to see a more serous side of the industry and cyber security as a whole. While we learned about the things which were said, now seeing it in practice with real life examples, really gave a depth and weight for everything we learned so far.

It was a great and eye opening experience, also managed to finish second place in the group competition.

Dublin tech tour
